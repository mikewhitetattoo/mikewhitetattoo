This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, stored, and protected when you visit or contact the Mike White Ink website, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

This Privacy Policy applies to visitors of this website and to individuals who voluntarily provide personal data through the contact form. The website is intended for informational and contact purposes only.

Personal data is collected only when voluntarily provided by the user. This may include name, email address, preferred appointment date and time, tattoo description, placement, and reference images.

Users are advised not to submit personal data beyond what is necessary to process their inquiry. Any information provided voluntarily is submitted at the user's own discretion.

This website and its services are intended for individuals aged 18 or older. Personal data of minors is not knowingly collected or processed.

Personal data is processed solely to respond to inquiries, discuss tattoo ideas, and manage appointments. Data is not used for marketing, analytics, or automated profiling.

The website owner reserves the right to ignore or delete inquiries that are clearly spam, abusive, or unrelated to the services provided.

Personal data is processed based on user consent and steps taken prior to entering into a service agreement, in accordance with Article 6(1)(a) and Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR.

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to handle communication and appointment-related matters, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

Users have the right to access their personal data, request correction or deletion, restrict processing, object to processing, and withdraw consent at any time.
