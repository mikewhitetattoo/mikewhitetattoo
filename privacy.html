Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, stored, and protected when you visit or contact the Mike White Ink website, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for the processing of personal data is:
Michal Plebanski, operating under the brand name Mike White
Tattoo Artist based in St. Paul’s Bay, Malta

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to visitors of this website and to individuals who voluntarily provide personal data through the contact form. The website is intended for informational and contact purposes only.

3. Personal Data Collected

Personal data is collected only when voluntarily provided by the user. This may include name, email address, preferred appointment date and time, tattoo description, placement, and reference images.

Users are advised not to submit personal data beyond what is necessary to process their inquiry. Any information provided voluntarily is submitted at the user's own discretion.

4. Age Limitation

This website and its services are intended for individuals aged 18 or older. Personal data of minors is not knowingly collected or processed. If personal data of a minor is identified, it will be deleted without undue delay.

5. Purpose of Processing

Personal data is processed solely to respond to inquiries, discuss tattoo ideas, and manage appointments. Data is not used for marketing, analytics, or automated profiling.

The website owner reserves the right to disregard or remove inquiries that are clearly spam, abusive, or unrelated to the services offered.

6. Legal Basis for Processing

Personal data is processed based on user consent and steps taken prior to entering into a service agreement, in accordance with Article 6(1)(a) and Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR.

7. Data Retention

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to handle communication and appointment-related matters, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses essential cookies and local storage only, which are required for basic website functionality and security. No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies are used. As only essential cookies are used, user consent is not required under applicable data protection regulations.

9. Third-Party Services and External Links

Personal data submitted through this website is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for commercial purposes. Contact form submissions are processed using a third-party service provider (Static Forms), which acts as a data processor and handles form data for the purpose of delivering messages to the website owner. Submitted data is subsequently delivered via email using Gmail services.

This website may contain links or embedded content from external platforms, including Instagram, WhatsApp, and Google Maps. These services operate independently and are governed by their own privacy policies.

The website owner has no control over the content, policies, or practices of third-party websites and is not responsible for any damages or issues arising from their use.

10. Data Security and Liability

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. The website owner cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted online.

11. Your Rights Under GDPR

Users have the right to access their personal data, request correction or deletion, restrict processing, object to processing, and withdraw consent at any time.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect legal or operational changes. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

13. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the processing of personal data, please contact Mike White Ink via the contact form on this website.
