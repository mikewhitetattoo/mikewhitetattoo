IN INK.

Based in St. Paul’s Bay, I’m a tattoo artist from Poland with over six years of experience. My work blends bold, clean linework with striking blackwork and Japanese-inspired motifs. I’m known for precision and attention to detail, creating tattoos that balance simplicity with impact, perfect for those who appreciate anime, manga, and minimalist yet expressive designs.

I draw inspiration from traditional tattooing, dark and blackwork aesthetics, as well as geek culture. While I’m recognized for bold, graphic styles, I also enjoy crafting delicate fineline pieces with the same level of care and precision.
