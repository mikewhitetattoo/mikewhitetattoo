COMMON QUESTIONS

General pricing

Tattoo prices typically start from €50 and can go up to €500, depending on the project.

What affects the price?

The final price depends on several factors, including the size of the tattoo, its placement on the body, the complexity of the design, level of detail, and the time required to complete the piece.

Price confirmation

The exact price will always be discussed and confirmed before the tattoo session begins. There are no hidden costs - you will know the full price in advance.

Deposit required

Yes, to book a tattoo session, a deposit is required. The deposit will be deducted from the final session price.

Deposit amounts

Tattoos up to €150: €40
Tattoos from €150 to €350: €80
Full-day session: €120

Deposit refund

The deposit is refundable only if the appointment is rescheduled or canceled at least 3 days before the planned session. Deposits for cancellations or changes made later than this period are non-refundable.

Final terms

The deposit is part of the final session price, not an additional fee. The appointment is only confirmed after the deposit has been paid.

Get a good night’s sleep

Make sure you are well-rested. Being tired can increase sensitivity to pain and make it harder to sit still during your session.

Eat before your session

Do not come on an empty stomach. Eating beforehand helps maintain energy levels and reduces the risk of dizziness.

Stay hydrated

Drink plenty of water the day before and the day of your session. Well-hydrated skin takes ink better and heals more easily.

Avoid alcohol, drugs, excess caffeine, and painkillers

Alcohol, recreational drugs, excess caffeine, and certain painkillers (such as aspirin or ibuprofen) can thin your blood. This may increase bleeding during tattooing and affect the healing process.

Wear comfortable clothing

Choose clothes that allow easy access to the area being tattooed and won’t restrict your movement.

Prepare your skin

Avoid tanning immediately before your session. Do not use harsh exfoliants or chemical treatments on the area to be tattooed. Arrive with clean, moisturized skin if possible.

Bring a positive attitude

Tattoo sessions can be long and require patience. Staying positive helps the process go smoothly and makes the experience more enjoyable.

Plan your post-tattoo time

Your skin may be red and sensitive after the session. Plan time to rest and avoid intense physical activity immediately afterward.

Leave the SecondSkin on

Keep the SecondSkin on for 24 - 48 hours, depending on your artist’s recommendation. It acts as a protective barrier and helps with faster healing.

Cleaning your tattoo

After removing the SecondSkin, wash your tattoo gently every 3 hours for the first day. Use lukewarm water and mild, fragrance-free soap. Pat dry gently with a clean paper towel or soft cloth.

Apply ointment

After washing, apply a thin layer of Bepanthen or another tattoo-specific healing ointment. Do not over-apply - just enough to lightly cover the tattoo. Important: Continue this routine for two weeks to ensure proper healing.

What should I avoid?

Avoid direct sunlight, swimming pools, the sea, and hot tubs for at least 2 weeks. Do not go to the gym or do intense workouts or activities that cause heavy sweating during this time. Excess sweat or friction can cause irritation, infection, and affect healing.

Itching during healing

It is normal for the tattoo to feel itchy while healing. Do not scratch, pick, or rub the tattoo, as this can damage the skin and affect the final result.

Ongoing care

Wear loose clothing to prevent rubbing. Keep the tattoo moisturized with Bepanthen or a tattoo-specific healing ointment until fully healed.

Leave the cling film on

Keep your tattoo covered with cling film for 24 - 48 hours, depending on your artist’s recommendation. During this period, replace the cling film every 3 hours to keep the tattoo clean and protected.

Cleaning your tattoo

Each time you remove the cling film, wash your tattoo gently with lukewarm water and mild, fragrance-free soap. Pat dry with a clean paper towel or soft cloth. Repeat this process every 3 hours together with the cling film changes.
